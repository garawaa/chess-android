Bishop

The Bishop has the ability to move diagonally anywhere. This makes them quite strong but they are limited to moving diagonally on their assigned colour square originally, meaning they are quite easy to counter if an opponent piece is being targetted. They are considered a minor piece, leveled with the knight because of this but can still be a very powerful piece.


For more information, check out the wiki page on Chess: Chess Wiki Page.
