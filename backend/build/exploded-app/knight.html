Knight

A Knight is the only piece that can jump over other pieces. It is unique in that while most other pieces move horizontally/vertically or diagonally, the knight moves in an L shape. This can make them very mobile when in the centre of the board because they can maneuver around pieces but have the problem of being cornered without much movement. They are considered a minor piece, leveled with the bishop.


For more information, check out the wiki page on Chess: Chess Wiki Page.
