Rook

The Rook has the ability to move horizontally and vertically anywhere. This makes them very strong because they can be in any position with ease and are not limited to certain squares. They cannot jump over other pieces. They are considered a major piece due to their power and is the second strongest piece, behind the queen.

The Rook also has a sub-ability called Castling where the King and rook move 1 square in and swap positions. To perform this move, the Bishop and Knight inbetween these pieces must be move out of their starting squares and no other piece must be in the way.


For more information, check out the wiki page on Chess: Chess Wiki Page.
