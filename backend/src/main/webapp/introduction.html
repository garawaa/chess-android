Introduction

Chess is a game of strategy, patience and critical thinking. The rules are simple but the outcomes are never-ending, making the game range from easy to hard.

There are 6 pieces in chess: King, Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight and Pawn

The aim of the game is to put the opponents King in check-mate. As you read this tutorial, you will learn about the various pieces, their abilities and importance and how to ultimately win a game of chess.


For more information, check out the wiki page on Chess: Chess Wiki Page.
